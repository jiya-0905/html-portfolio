My top 3 movies of all time

Nikka Zaildar

Every movie of this series is an amazing comedy thriller. Every punjabi movie lover has at least once watched this movie.

Sultan

This is the story of a world champion in wrestling from being a nobody to a world class player to again a nobody and then bouncing back to life for his purpose and lady love. Starring Anushka Sharma and Salman Khan , this movie is an emotional roller coaster.">

RRR

This Telegu film starring Ram Charan and Jr.NTR is a story on friendship and patriotism that touched the heart of every indian who watched it. And of course ,how can we forget the Oscar award winning song Naatu-Naatu that made the whole world groove on its tune. This movie is a must watch.
